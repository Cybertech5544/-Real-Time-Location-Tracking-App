Real-Time Location Tracker

This application shows your current location on a map and allows you to see the locations of other users in real-time using peer-to-peer technology.

How to Use

  1. Allow location access when prompted by your browser
  2. Copy your Connection ID and share it with friends
  3. Have friends enter your ID in the "Connect to Peer ID" field and click Connect
  4. Alternatively, click "Create Room" to generate a room ID that multiple people can join
  5. See everyone's locations update in real-time on the map
