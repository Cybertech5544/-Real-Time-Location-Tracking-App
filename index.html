How to Use

  1. Allow location access when prompted by your browser
  2. Copy your Connection ID and share it with friends
  3. Have friends enter your ID in the "Connect to Peer ID" field and click Connect
  4. Toggle "Show Movement Paths" to see trails behind each user
  5. Enable "Snap to Roads" for more accurate road-based tracking
  6. See everyone's locations and movement paths update in real-time
